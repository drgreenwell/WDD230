Lone Star Rallycross

Lone Star RallyCross is a time trial multi surface racing series that consists of 2 wheel drive and all wheel drive vehicles. This series brings affordable events, simple rules, quality competition and an emphasis on having fun. Lone Star RallyCross is based out of Brianne Corn Raceway, the states only purpose built rallycross facility. Our goal is to make rally racing affordable and accessible to everyone.

Date: March 23, 2024
